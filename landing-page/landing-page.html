Patient Monitoring System

Ateneo de Davao University - School of Nursing

A system for
managing and monitoring the patients of the
Ateneo de Davao University - School of Nursing students

Patient Management

This monitoring system allows users to manage their patients. This includes adding of new patients, updating of patient information, and removing of relevant information.

Sensor Management

Other than the monitoring of patients, this system permits users to manage sensors. This includes the adding, updating, and removing of sensors necessary.

Report Generation

Through the use of the monitoring system, Ateneo de Davao University - School of Nursing faculty are now able to easily keep track of the progress of their students during their hospital duties.
